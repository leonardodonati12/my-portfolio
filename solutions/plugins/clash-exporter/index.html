Quick Clash Exporter

The Quick Clash Exporter bridges the gap between raw Navisworks collision detection and actionable project management data. Standard HTML reports from Navisworks are often difficult to parse; this plugin extracts all clash data directly into structured Excel formats and Power BI-ready datasets.

It automatically pulls associated metadata, grid locations, and custom viewports, allowing project managers to visualize clash density and assign responsibilities dynamically without opening the BIM model.
