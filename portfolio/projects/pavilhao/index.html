Pavilhăo Paramétrico

An exploration of algorithmic design, this pavilion was generated using purely computational logic to optimize solar shading and material efficiency. By inputting environmental data into Grasshopper, the form organically adapts to the sun path, creating a dynamic interplay of light and shadow.

The structure is composed of digitally fabricated interlocking modules, designed to be assembled and disassembled without the need for complex mechanical fasteners, pushing the boundaries of sustainable, temporary architecture.
